---
title: "Why context windows matter more than you think"
date: "2026-04-28"
description: "Everyone optimizes the prompt. Almost nobody thinks about what else is in the context window — and that's where most of the performance gets left on the table."
---

Everyone focuses on the prompt. The prompt is the instruction. But the context window is the workspace — it's everything Claude can see at once.

Most people using Claude in E&P are filling maybe 10% of it. The other 90% is available, and using it well is the difference between a generic answer and one that actually fits your situation.
